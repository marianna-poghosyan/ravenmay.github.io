MineLife.org
Category : Website
Client : Brian McGann
Completion : Oct 2018
Role : Graphic Designer & UI/UX Designer

MineLife is a free analytical financial and geological search engine for investors interested in comparing and locating publicly listed mining companies with extensive and easy to use filters. This tool serves as an enabler to the investment community by allowing you to discover the mining company you want, on your terms.

I was in charge of the graphic design and also UI/UX design of this website, hired by Bria n Mcgann - CEO and founder of minelife.org. It was easy to collaborate and worked with other team members. The project was completed successfully.
