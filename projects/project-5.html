CSGOFrog.com
Category : Website
Client : Damien Gensanne
Completion : March 2019
Role : Frontend Developer

CsGoFrog is a service, where participants pay in their items (skins), and when we have 100 skins in total the system randomly determines one winner, who gets all deposited items.

The Client wanted to redesign their old website to make it look more professional and modern looking. I was hired individually to design and develop this whole website. The mock-ups of the new design was partially provided.
